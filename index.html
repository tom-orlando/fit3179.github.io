Which boroughs are most expensive?

New York's Borough #1 (Manhattan) is by far the most prestigious and expensive of the five. You can see for yourself using the filtering feature in the adjacent bubble plot.

This fact is due to the large office buildings and commercial property in iconic spots like Wall St and Times Square. Residential property around Central Park is also not cheap!
